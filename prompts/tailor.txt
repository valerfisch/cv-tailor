1. A job posting analysis (JSON)

3. User preferences from an interactive Q&A session

  - The core experience entries (most recent and most relevant) are always included
  - Maximum 2 projects with 2 bullets each
  - Maximum 2 education entries (one line each)

- You MAY lightly reframe bullet text to better match posting keywords, but do NOT fabricate experience or metrics.
- NEVER invent skills, technologies, or techniques not present in the master CV. Only use items that actually appear in the master CV data. For example, do NOT expand "Prompt Engineering" into "Chain of Thought Prompting" or add frameworks/tools the candidate hasn't listed.
- NEVER use em dashes (—) to insert mid-sentence asides or clauses. Use separate sentences instead.
- Choose the best title variant or write a custom one that matches the posting.
- Write a custom summary (2-3 lines) tailored to this specific role.
- Order skill categories by relevance to the posting.
- For each skill category, pick only the items most relevant to the posting (max ~6-8 items, must fit one line).
